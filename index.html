60-80
100-120
150-200

2. Why is early CPR critical in a cardiac emergency?

It maintains blood flow to vital organs
it's a legal requirement
It restarts the heart instantly every time

3. Why should you perform CPR immediately after a cardiac arrest?

Makes sure the blood begins to flow as soon as possible
Makes sure they keep their memories
Reduces probability of a heart attack

4. What is the probability of reducing mortality after doing CPR?

50%
75%
80%
